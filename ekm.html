ERNAKULAM

Ernakulam is a district of Kerala, India. Situated in the central part of the state, spanning an area of about 3,000 square kilometres (1,200 sq mi), Ernakulam district is home to over 9% of Kerala's population. Its headquarters is located at Kakkanad. Ernakulam district includes Kochi city, which is known as the IT and commercial capital of Kerala.

Population

According to the 2018 Statistics Report Ernakulam district has a population of 3,427,659 roughly equal to the nation of Uruguay or the US states of Utah or Iowa.According to the 2011 Census of India, district is ranked 104th most populous in India, out of a total of 640. The district has a population density of 1,072 inhabitants per square kilometre (2,780/sq mi). Its population growth rate over the decade 2001–2011 was 5.69%. Ernakulam has a sex ratio of 1027 females for every 1000 males, and a literacy rate of 95.89%.
